Agricultural Management Systems

  • Systems for the control and management of animals and plants found and traded within and outside Brazilian states.

    My role in these systems is in data migration, maintenance and support, as well as the implementation of new features requested by the respective states. The system was built on Zend2 currently running with PHP 7.4.

    Working with SIGADERR, SIDAAM and other systems, I learned more deeply about the PHP language and database modeling, in addition to gaining more experience in the relationship with the end user. The systems are used by thousands of users in different states, this brings a great responsibility in the planning and development of new features.
